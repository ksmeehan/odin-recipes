Okonomiyaki

Description

Also known as the Japanese savoury pancake

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon dashi powder
  • 60ml Flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 60ml Water
  • 1 egg
  • 150g shredded cabbage
  • 2 thinly sliced spring onions
  • 3 pieces shaved pork belly
  • A pinch of Aonori (tiny seaweed flakes), for toppings
  • A few pieces of pickled ginger, for toppings
  • Okonomi sauce, for toppings
  • Kewpie mayonnaise, for toppings
  • Bonito flakes, for toppings

Steps

  1. Put pan on to heat.
  2. Combine dashi, flour and baking powder.
  3. Add water and egg and combine.
  4. Add spring onions and cabbage.
  5. Toss until the batter is all through the cabbage.
  6. Add oil to pan, and then arrange the batter on the pan in a pancake shape.
  7. Cook, covered, for 4-5 minutes.
  8. Place shaved pork belly atop okonomiyaki and flip with a large spatula.
  9. Cook for another 4-5 minutes.
  10. Take of heat and decorate with mayonnaise, okonomi sauce, aonori, bonito flakes and pickled ginger.
