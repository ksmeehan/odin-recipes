Beef Mince

Description

Hearty beef mince -- this simple beef mince recipe is comes with Italian and Mexican options.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 shredded carrot
  • 2 garlic cloves (chopped or minced)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • olive oil
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste or 1/2 jar passata
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • salt
  • pepper
  • If Cooking Italian: use herbs like thyme, rosemary, ground nutmeg
  • If cooking Mexican: use spices like paprica, cumin

Steps

  1. Prepare all ingredients as above.
  2. Fry onions in il, add garlic in the last minute or so.
  3. Add carrot, cook another 2 minutes.
  4. Add beef and break up clumps. Fry until slightly coloured.
  5. Add tomato paste + water or passata
  6. Add beef stock and worstershire sauce
  7. Add seasonings according to mexican or italian style
  8. Cook for 1/2 an hour or so, stirring every few minutes
