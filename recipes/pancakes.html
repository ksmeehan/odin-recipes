Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Fluffy, delcious, simple pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 cup self-raising flower
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Butter or oil, for frying

Steps

  1. Mix flour and sugar together in a bowl & make a well in the middle.
  2. Ppour eggs, milk and vanilla essence into the well. Mix lightly so as not to disturb the well.
  3. Mix wet and dry ingredients together until just combined.
  4. (Optional) Let batter rest for half an hour.
  5. Heat pan.
  6. Grease pan with oil.
  7. Scoop about 1/4 of batter into the pan
  8. Cook pancakes for 2 minutes each side
